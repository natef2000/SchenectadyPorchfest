Like What You're Hearing?

Schenectady Porchfest relies on community sponsors and volunteers to bring live music to your doorstep -- literally! 2025 marks only the third ever porchfest event organized in Schenectady. The tradition is still young, but we hope to keep it going for years to come! Your contributions help tremendously in ensuring the continuation of this wonderful event. Thank you for your support!
