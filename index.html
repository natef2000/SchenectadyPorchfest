Like what you're hearing? Schenectady Porchfest relies on community sponsors and volunteers to bring live music to your doorstep -- literally! 2025 marks only the third porchfest event organized in Schenectady. The tradition is still young, but we hope to keep it going for years to come! If you'd like to support the continuation of this wonderful event, please consider donating to our non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. Your contribution would help tremendously. Thank you for your support!
